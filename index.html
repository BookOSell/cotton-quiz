Introduction

About The Fest

This intellectual festival brings together curious minds, aspiring leaders, passionate speakers, and competitive thinkers.

Through challenging quiz competitions and engaging public speaking workshops, participants will sharpen their knowledge, communication skills, confidence, and leadership abilities. Cotton University prides itself on fostering academic and intellectual excellence, and this fest is the arena for students to showcase their genius.

FLAGSHIP CONTEST

Quiz Competition

Test your knowledge across diverse domains, compete with the brightest minds, and showcase your analytical thinking.

  • Multiple rounds: Preliminary written test, semi-finals, and dynamic audio-visual stage finals.
  • Team participation: Form a team of up to 3 members to crack testing brain teasers.
  • Exciting prizes: Trophies, merit certificates, and cash pools for top 3 teams.
  • Syllabus: General knowledge, current affairs, pop culture, science, tech, history, and North-East India heritage.

Public Speaking Workshop

Learn the art of confident communication, persuasive speaking, stage presence, and audience engagement from experienced mentors.

  • Interactive sessions: Hands-on speaking labs with personalized direct feedback.
  • Confidence-building: Battle stage anxiety with science-backed breathing & posture methods.
  • Speech structuring: Master hooks, arguments, and calls to action using classic narrative frames.
  • Practical activities: Impromptu speech sessions (Jam sessions) and mock debates.
